In this section we will look at the soft requirements they ask for in Atos:

1. Teamwork & Collaboration

Ability to work in cross-functional teams (designers, developers, project managers, etc.). Strong interpersonal skills to communicate effectively with colleagues and stakeholders.

2. Effective Communication

Clear articulation of technical concepts to non-technical audiences. Proficiency in English (often required for international projects).

3. Adaptability & Continuous Learning

Willingness to learn new technologies and adapt to different project environments. Flexibility to work with diverse clients and evolving business needs.

4. Problem-Solving & Critical Thinking

Analytical mindset to troubleshoot and optimize web solutions. Proactive approach to finding efficient and innovative solutions.
